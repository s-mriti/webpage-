Our Story

Bridging India &
The Gulf Through
Wellness

IndoGulf Yoga & Wellness was founded on a simple conviction: authentic Indian wellness traditions — yoga, breathwork, mindful movement — deserve a world-class home in Bahrain. We serve the Indian expat community, GCC nationals, and wellness seekers of all backgrounds across Manama.

Our certified male and female coaches bring decades of experience across yoga, aquatics, and holistic health. From children taking their first strokes, to corporate professionals unwinding, to experienced yogis deepening their practice — we have a program crafted for you.

Why IndoGulf

What Sets Us
Apart in
the GCC

In a region filled with wellness options, IndoGulf delivers something rare: authentic Indian wellness philosophy fused with world-class coaching — designed for Bahrain's diverse, multinational community.

01

Indo-Gulf Cultural Bridge

We understand the Indian expat experience in the GCC deeply — with cultural sensibilities that feel like home, while serving all nationalities with equal warmth and professionalism.

02

Certified Male & Female Coaches

Both male and female certified instructors ensure comfort and cultural sensitivity for all — including ladies-only yoga and ladies-only swimming sessions.

03

All Ages, Every Level

From toddlers learning to float, to seniors in gentle yoga, to cabin crew completing water safety certification — our programs span every stage of life.

04

Gulf-Friendly Timings

Early morning (5 AM–11 AM) and evening (5 PM–7 PM) batches daily — designed around Bahrain's working hours, prayer times, and the summer heat.

05

Diplomatic Area Location

Centrally located in Manama's prestigious Diplomatic Area — accessible from Seef, Juffair, Adliya, Budaiya, Saar, and all major residential zones.
